REC Class

Learn from basic, start from zero

REC Class is a series of lectures conducted by REC Engineering Team annually, aimed to provide all students in Universiti Malaya the knowledge of programming, mechanical and electronics. Topics ranging from arduino, to turning a motor, to register programming etc. 

Previ​ous REC Class (2021)
Time: 2 hours per week, 8 weeks

Fee: NONE

platform: ZOOM & TEAMS

Participants: first year ee students & others
After rec class, we learnt
- Arduino basicS
- input and output
- servo motor
- Mechanical
- schematic
- programming
- pcb introduction
- CONTROLLER
- 3D DESIGN & PRINTING
PCB Workshop 2021
through the via, route the trace

PCB Workshop was a 3-days workshops conducted by REC PCB Engineering Team. Participants got to hands-on with schematic and printed circuit board (PCB) design using Altium software. At the end of the workshop, everyone designed their own stream deck.
